# Accessibility Notes

Lineform includes accessibility as core product behavior.

## Current Coverage

- Keyboard-accessible menu commands.
- Native text editing with standard selection, undo, spell checking, and find behavior.
- Reading Experience presets for focus, dyslexia-friendly settings, low vision, and low light.
- Adjustable font family, font size, line spacing, margins, themes, caret width, typewriter mode, and reading ruler.
- Native preview and outline views.
- Accessible diff navigation for intelligent editing suggestions.

## Remaining Manual Checks

- VoiceOver navigation across toolbar controls.
- Keyboard-only acceptance and rejection of suggestions.
- Large-text and high-contrast review on a physical Mac display.
